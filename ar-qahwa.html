غلّي الماي
Pour 3 cups of fresh, filtered water into a clean dallah. Place it over medium heat and bring it to a rolling boil. The quality of the water matters — Najdi hosts always use the purest water available.
💡 Cultural Note: The dallah is never washed with soap — only rinsed with hot water. The residue from years of brewing adds depth to the flavor.
