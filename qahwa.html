The Majlis — المجلس

You step into a traditional Najdi majlis in Riyadh. The room is carpeted, low-lit, and fragrant with oud incense. Men sit cross-legged in a circle. Before you say a single word, a host approaches with a gleaming dallah.

In Najd, silence is a guest. The coffee speaks first.

The Dallah — الدلة

The dallah is not just a coffee pot — it is an ancient social signal. In Najdi culture, the type of dallah, its size, and how it is held all communicate the host's status and intention before coffee is even poured.

A gold-trimmed dallah means you are an honored guest. A plain one means you are family — equally respected, but differently.

The Order — الترتيب

Before any guest drinks, the host must know the order of service. In a Najdi majlis, coffee always flows from the eldest to the youngest. Skipping an elder — even by accident — is a social insult that echoes through families for years.

قاعدة ذهبية
"الكبير أولاً — ثم الضيف الأعز — ثم البقية"
The eldest first — then the honored guest — then the rest

The Pour — الصب

Coffee is poured with the left hand holding the dallah and the right hand offering the cup. The cup — called a finjan — is always small. And it is never filled more than a quarter.

A full cup says: "I want you to leave quickly."
A quarter cup says: "Stay as long as you wish."

How to Brew Najdi Qahwa

1
Roast lightly — التحميص
Lightly roast green coffee beans — don't over-roast, they stay pale gold.
حمّص الحبوب خفيف، تبقى لونها ذهبي فاتح
2
Grind with hal & saffron — الطحن
Grind coarsely with cardamom pods and a pinch of saffron.
طحن خشن مع هيل وزعفران
3
Fill the dallah — التعبئة
Add to the dallah with cold water — about one spoon per cup.
حط في الدلة مع ماء بارد، ملعقة لكل فنجان
4
Simmer gently — الغلي
Simmer on low heat for 10–15 minutes — never boil hard.
غلي هادئ ١٠-١٥ دقيقة، ما تفور
5
Strain through the filter — التصفية
Strain through the dallah's built-in filter into a second dallah.
صفي من دلة لدلة
6
Pour a quarter finjan — الصب
Pour only a quarter of the finjan — a full cup means 'leave now'.
صب ربع الفنجان بس
